About CraWings

CraWings Pizza Shop began as a small idea between friends who shared a love for great pizza and bringing people together through food. What started as casual pizza nights quickly turned into a dream of opening a place where fresh, handcrafted pizza could be enjoyed by the whole community. From the beginning, CraWings focused on using simple, high-quality ingredients and preparing dough fresh every day.

The restaurant celebrates different pizza traditions from around the world. The menu features six unique styles of pizza, each with its own character and flavor. The Grandma Pizza is known for its thin crust and rich tomato sauce, while the Neapolitan-Style Pizza offers a soft, wood-fired crust with fresh mozzarella and basil. The thick and fluffy Sicilian Pizza brings a classic square slice with bold flavor.

CraWings also serves the crispy and light Roman-Style Pizza, the deep and hearty Quad City-Style Pizza, and the creative California-Style Pizza, which highlights fresh toppings and unique combinations. Each style reflects a different tradition of pizza-making while keeping the CraWings commitment to quality and flavor.

Today, CraWings Pizza Shop continues to share its passion for pizza by bringing together these different styles in one place, giving customers the chance to explore classic recipes and new favorites in a welcoming environment.
